Biography

I am currently a fourth-year (2019-now) Ph.D. student in the IMAGINE/LIGM team, the Ecole des Ponts ParisTech, under the supervision of Prof. Vincent Lepetit. My research interests lie primarily in computer vision. I have done some projects in 2D object detection/segmentation in images/videos, tracking and depth estimation.
